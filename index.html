"The Curse of the Abandoned Farmhouse"

The sun was setting on the horizon as I drove into the small town. I had been searching for answers for weeks and finally, I thought I had found the answer.

I pulled up to the old abandoned farmhouse and stepped out of the car. The once beautiful farmhouse was now in ruins, with broken windows and overgrown weeds. I was almost too scared to go in, but I was determined to find out what had happened here.

I stepped inside and instantly felt a chill run down my spine. I could feel the presence of something sinister in the air. I slowly made my way through the house, searching for clues.

Suddenly I heard a noise coming from upstairs. I froze in place, not sure if I should investigate or not. I knew I had to make a decision right away, so I either had to go upstairs to investigate the noise or stay put and wait to see what happened.
